About DayZ

DayZ is a survival video game developed and published by Bohemia Interactive. The game places the player in the fictional post-Soviet state of Chernarus, where an unknown virus has turned most of the population into violent zombies. The player must scavenge for food, water, weapons, and medicine while trying to survive against both the zombies and other players.

The game was first released in December 2013 as an early access title on Steam, and was later released for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in 2019. The game has received mixed reviews, with praise for its immersive gameplay and criticism for its bugs and glitches.
